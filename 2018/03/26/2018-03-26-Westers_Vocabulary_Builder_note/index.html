    2) N-COUNT: The beneficiaries of a will legally receive money or property from someone when that person dies.

    In those financially desperate years, the young couple was saved only by the benevolence of her elderly great-uncle.

    paramours
    N-COUNT: oft poss N Someone’s paramour is their lover. [OLD-FASHIONED]

    2) ADJ-GRADED: usu ADJ n If someone has pacifist views, they believe that war and violence are always wrong.

    pacts

    egomaniacs

    N-UNCOUNT

    regencies

    1) ADJ: usu ADJ n Regency is used to refer to the period in Britain at the beginning of the nineteenth century, and to the style of architecture, literature, and furniture that was popular at the time.
    2) N-COUNT A regency is a period of time when a country is governed by a regent, because the king or queen is unable to rule.

      The latest blood test had revealed that her hematocrit had risen considerably and was almost back to normal.
